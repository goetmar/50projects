Why monogreen wurms is the best deck for new commander players

by Marco Göttgens

When you invite a new player to a game of Commander to get them familiar with the format, the question arises what deck to give them. After all, you want them to have fun. In my experience, "Baru, Wurmspeaker" has everything you need to provide that.

First of all, the mono-green color of the deck keeps it simple. Learning to play just one color emphasizes its strengths and abilities without having to balance your mana production. Reducing the land diversity down to a mix of basics with a few utility lands allows you to focus on the fun of casting impactful spells. Thankfully, spell selection in green is straight forward: you have sorceries to draw cards and ramp up your mana, instants to interact with the board and big creatures to win by combat. Ramp is very powerful in Commander and really enables your gameplan of playing expensive cards.

Wurms play right into the core mechanics of green, as they themselves are huge cretures by definition. They don't really have any defining characteristics other than their unusually large bodies, which leads to a variety of unique and sometimes wonky mechanics that each wurm brings to the table. The archetype has a mysterious flavor to, as wurms are related to dragons but feel more primal.

"Baru, Wurmspeaker" as the commander offers a pump effect paired with trample for all your wurms, which is just a great and strong effect to have sitting in the command zone. Together with an affordable mana cost and the ability to generate wurms himself, he makes for well-designed commander.

Ultimately this deck brings joy by combining the simple and strong mechanics of green with the sheer power and excitement of wurm creatures as the commander glues the two together. If this speaks to you, try it out for yourself or share it with friends who are new to the format!

I didn't talk about specific cards in this article, as I wanted to keep it more general. If you are interested, you can check out my version of the deck here: Cute Wurms

Baru, Wurmspeaker

Baru provides an anthem effect for your wurms and gives them trample, which is essential for large creatures. He can also generate a 4/4 wurm for 1 green mana, if you have a wurm with base power 5 or more in play.
